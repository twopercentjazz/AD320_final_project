About The Gold Room

Step into the Gold Room, the Overlook Hotel's crown jewel, where elegance and history blend to create an unforgettable experience. Immerse yourself in the opulence of the 1920s, as this grand ballroom transports you to an era of glamour and festivity. The Gold Room at the Overlook Hotel isn't just a venue; it's a step back in time, an experience that captures the essence of an era defined by extravagance and joy. Join us for an evening of impeccable drinks, exquisite dining, and entertainment that will transport you to the heart of the Jazz Age. Experience the allure and charm of the 1920s in a setting that's as timeless as it is enchanting.

Libations

In the heart of the Gold Room, our speakeasy-style bar serves a tantalizing array of classic cocktails. Crafted by expert mixologists, our drinks menu features timeless favorites like the Manhattan and the Martini, alongside our signature creations inspired by the Roaring Twenties. Cuisine

Our dinner service in the Gold Room is a culinary journey through time. Executive Chef Emily Carter has masterfully created a menu that revives the spirit and flavors of the 1920s with a modern twist. Indulge in luxurious dishes such as Oysters Rockefeller, Chateaubriand, and Waldorf Salad, all prepared with locally sourced ingredients and served on fine china. The opulent setting, complete with crystal chandeliers and gold-leaf accents, provides the perfect backdrop for a sumptuous dining experience.

Entertainment

As night falls, the Gold Room comes alive with the sounds of jazz and swing. Our stage has hosted some of the most renowned jazz musicians of the era, and today, we continue this tradition. Dance the night away to a live band playing the hits of the 1920s, from Duke Ellington to Louis Armstrong, in an atmosphere charged with excitement and nostalgia. The grand piano, a centerpiece of the room, often features surprise performances, adding to the magic of your evening.
