Hedge Maze

About The Overlook Maze

The Overlook Hotel's legendary hedge maze, a marvel of landscape architecture and a centerpiece of our historic grounds, invites guests to embark on a journey through its verdant, meticulously crafted corridors. The Overlook's hedge maze is not just a feature of our hotel; it's a journey into the heart of tranquility and mystery. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned explorer, the maze beckons with the promise of discovery and the allure of the Overlook's storied past. Join us, and lose yourself in the enchantment of this extraordinary garden masterpiece.

Design

Designed by renowned landscape artist Edward Carver, the maze spans over three acres, with walls of lush greenery standing over six feet high. Its design is inspired by the grand mazes of European estates, yet with a distinctive twist that reflects the unique spirit of the Overlook. As guests wander its paths, they encounter a series of enchanting surprises: serene resting nooks, sculptures hidden amongst the foliage, and occasional glimpses of the hotel's majestic fa√ßade.

History

Conceived in the early 20th century, the maze's history is as rich and intriguing as the hotel itself. Commissioned by the original owner, Gerald Fordham, it was intended as a tranquil retreat for guests seeking solitude and inspiration. Over the decades, it has been the site of numerous high-profile events, including garden parties, literary gatherings, and even a few cinematic shoots.
