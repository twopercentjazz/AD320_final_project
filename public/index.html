Rooms & Suites

Our rooms are spacious and comfortable, with breathtaking views of the Rocky Mountains. Our suites are perfect for families or groups of friends who want to enjoy the beauty of nature while still having all the comforts of home. So whether you're looking for a romantic weekend getaway or just want to get away from it all, we have something for everyone at the Overlook Hotel. Come play with us!

Dinner & Drinks

Visit the Gold Room for a fine dining experience you won't soon forget. You'll be treated like royalty from the moment you walk through our doors until the moment you leave. If you're looking to take your mind off of work, stop by our bar where we serve up the best cocktails in town, you might just want to stay forever and ever.

Overlook Maze

During your stay make sure to check out our famous hedge maze! Our outdoor labyrinth is the perfect place to get lost in nature and forget about all your worries. The maze will keep you entertained for hours as you try to find your way out. It's also a great place to spend time with family.
